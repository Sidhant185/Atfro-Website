B2B SaaS • Growth Architecture

Operational Scaling for a High-Growth Platform

Problem. A high-growth SaaS platform was running on disconnected tools, manual lead routing, and ad-hoc reporting. Leadership lacked a single view of pipeline health and operational capacity.

Architecture. We designed and implemented a unified CRM, rebuilt funnels end-to-end, and established a brand-consistent narrative across all touchpoints so sales, marketing, and product spoke the same language.

  • 3x increase in qualified pipeline velocity within 6 months.
  • 100% reduction in manual data entry errors.
  • Single, shared dashboard for leadership and revenue teams.

Consumer Brands • Digital Experience

Redefining Premium Engagement for Sattvahar
