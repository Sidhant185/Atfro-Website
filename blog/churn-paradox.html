Growth Engineering

The Churn Paradox.

Why onboarding is your most important marketing channel.

Most companies focus 90% of their energy on top-of-funnel acquisition, only to lose those customers due to poor activation. The "Churn Paradox" is the reality that the more you spend on acquisition without a solid onboarding architecture, the faster you lose revenue.

Onboarding as a Growth Lever

We flip the script. The most effective growth lever in your architecture is actually your customer experience post-sign-up. By designing systems that reduce Time-to-Value (TTV), you create compounding loops of retention and referral.

Systems of Activation

Activation isn't a one-time event; it's a structural process. From triggered education to behavioral nudges, your onboarding architecture should guide the customer effortlessly toward their first "Aha!" moment. Fix your activation, and your acquisition spend becomes exponentially more efficient.
